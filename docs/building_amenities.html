Features & Amenities

Rooftop Pool & Terrace
The rooftop pool & terrace is the heart of the MidCity Lofts community. Neighbors often share memorable days in the sun while gazing out on the spectacular skyline views.


Penthouse Sky Lounge & Fitness Center
The glass enclosed penthouse lounge & fitness center feature stunning 360 degree skyline views. Residents frequently reserve the lounge for private parties & events that require a distinctive urban chic vibe. A conference room & business facilities are also available for resident use.


24 Hour Concierge & Security
Whether it´s a friendly hello as you walk in the door, or a convenient call to let you know that a package, or expected guest has arrived. The 24 hour concierge is always on staff to help
