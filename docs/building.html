MidCity Lofts

Completed in 2002, MidCity Lofts is the epitome of well-executed modern design. Native materials such as red brick and granite surround the exterior and allow the building to be modern without screaming "new."


Walls of glass highlight the north and south entrances, forming a welcoming view into the building that begs for passersby to peek inside. The discreet design allows the top 7 floors to seemingly float weightlessly above the base.


Inside, the lofts feature floor-to-ceiling windows, covered balconies, designer kitchens and exposed surfaces while consciously avoiding the urban loft cliche of exposed ductwork and conduit.


Topped with a rooftop pool and a glass enclosed penthouse lounge, MidCity Lofts offers some of the most striking views of the city available.
